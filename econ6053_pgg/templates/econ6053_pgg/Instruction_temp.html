In this study, you will be in a randomly formed group of {{ Constants.players_per_group }} participants. Participants will be in the same group through all rounds of the experiment.

At the beginning of each round, all participants in the group are given an endowment of {{ Constants.endowment }}, and have the opportunity to contribute to a joint project. Contributions can be any amount from 0 to their endowment ({{ Constants.endowment }} in this case).

The earnings from the project are calculated as follows: The total contribution of all participants is multiplied by a factor of {{ Constants.factor }}, and the resulting amount is the total earnings from the project, which is evenly split among all {{ Constants.players_per_group }} participants.

The experiment has in total {{ Constants.num_rounds }} rounds. We will randomly choose one round to pay you, your payoff equals your earnings from the project, plus the amount you did not contribute in that round.
